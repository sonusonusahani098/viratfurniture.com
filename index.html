Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about working with Virat Furniture. Still have a question? Reach out on WhatsApp or call us directly.

Which areas in Surat does Virat Furniture serve?

We focus on Surat's premium residential neighbourhoods, including Vesu, Adajan, Pal and Gaurav Path Road, working from our base in Adajan.

Do you provide a free site visit and estimate?

Yes. Share your project details through our contact form or WhatsApp, and our team will arrange a free site visit and estimate within 24 hours.

What type of furniture does Virat Furniture make?

We design and build custom beds, almari and wardrobes, modular kitchens, main gates, study and office units, storage solutions, living room furniture and bathroom vanities — all made to measure.

Is the furniture custom-made or off-the-shelf?

Every piece is 100% custom made for your space. We do not sell catalogue or pre-fabricated furniture — each design is built to your exact measurements and style.

How experienced is the Virat Furniture team?

Virat Furniture has been crafting furniture for Surat homes since 2008, with a team of 16 skilled carpenters, designers and installation specialists.
